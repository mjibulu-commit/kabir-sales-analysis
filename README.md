Based on yearly performance, trend analysis shows that the trend of this business performance is not regular. 2010 being the first year had a reasonable performance then there came 2011 in which most of the reps had higher performance compared to the previous year except Ayo who had nearly a third of his starting year performance. In the final given year (2012), more than half of the representatives had reduced revenue up to half of their second year. Only one rep (Abdul) managed to improve his revenue all year long.
---
This company appear to be under performing, therefore, hiring more staff is unlikely to imrove their revenue or performance. There focus should be how to improve on the ones they already have or possibly downsize.
